Values

The first thing you do when creating your profile at Essentialsim is determine what your values are. Whether that be getting fit, saving money, or getting a raise. Once your values are set, the magic starts to work, your daily list of todos will be prioritized to ensure you are focused on what matters!

Projects

Not only can you prioritize your daily activities, you can also implement larger projects. Whether that be building that new shed in the backyard, or finishing that assignment at work you have procrastinating on.
